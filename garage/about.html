About Us

Passion for Performance. Precision in Every Detail.

Who we are

fountics Garage is more than just a service center — it’s a hub for car enthusiasts, everyday drivers, and performance seekers who demand the best for their vehicles. Founded on a commitment to technical excellence, transparency, and innovation, we offer an experience that blends motorsport-level precision with personalized customer care.

Our team of certified technicians brings decades of hands-on experience working with Japanese and other Asian car brands. Whether it’s routine maintenance, diagnostics, or performance upgrades, we treat every vehicle as if it were our own — delivering results that go beyond expectation.

Our Team

At fountics, we’ve handpicked professionals who live and breathe automotive engineering. Our staff undergoes continuous training in the latest automotive technologies and diagnostic tools, ensuring that your car receives care aligned with global service standards.

Our Facility

We operate in a state-of-the-art facility featuring fully equipped service bays, digital alignment systems, and performance testing tools. From the moment you arrive, you'll find a welcoming customer lounge with modern amenities and real-time service transparency through our open-bay design and live updates.
