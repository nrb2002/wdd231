About FOUNTICS

FOUNTICS is a dedicated Computer Science Research Center committed to advancing the frontiers of technology through research, innovation, and education. Our interdisciplinary teams explore topics ranging from artificial intelligence and cybersecurity to data analytics and software engineering.
