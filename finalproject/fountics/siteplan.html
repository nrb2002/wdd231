Purpose

The website presents fountics - Garage, a modern auto service startup specializing in maintenance, repairs, and diagnostics for Asian car brands—particularly Japanese makes like Toyota, Hyundai, Honda, Nissan, Mazda, and Subaru.


Scenarios

  • How can I get the price of a bulk product for my company's fleet?
